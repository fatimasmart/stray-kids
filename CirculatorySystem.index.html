  • About

    • The circulatory system is like a highway for your body, made up of the heart, blood vessels (arteries, veins, and capillaries), and blood. Its main job is to transport oxygen and nutrients to every cell in your body and to carry waste products away. Think of the heart as the pump that pushes blood, the arteries as roads that take blood away from the heart, and the veins as roads that bring blood back to the heart.
